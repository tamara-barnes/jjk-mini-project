Domain Expansion

Domain Expansion - the most powerful technique. Because it allows sourcerers or cursed spirits to project their inner psyche to a trapped space and imbue it with their cursed technique, it creates a insanely powerful enviornment where their attacks are guaranteed to hit

Each Domain Expansion is unique with their own strengths and characteristics.

Gojo Satoru - Unlimited Void

Itadori Yuji - Benevolent Shrine

Yuta Okkotsu - Authentic Mutal Love

Megumi Fushiguro - Chimera Shadow Garden
